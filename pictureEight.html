sakshi harjani

on top of the world

grouse mountain, vancouver, canada

april 2, 2018

this was the first time the photographer went hiking. the fact that it was in the snow scared her but looking out over the high hills of this beautiful mountain was worth it. with this image, she wanted to display the grand piece of land in front of her, as well as the way in which the sky fades into a darker blue.
